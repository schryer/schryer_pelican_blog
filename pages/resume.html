Resume

Researcher — Technical editor — Programmer — FOSS activist

Systems biology modeler with a chemical engineering background. Specialty in dynamic isotope exchange experiments to study both metabolic flux and protein production and turnover. Capable communicator (founder of Scientific Scribe OÜ) with modeling and programming skills. Believes that reproducible research is best realized using free and open source (FOSS) tools.
